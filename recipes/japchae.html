Japchae

Description

Japchae is a savory and slightly sweet dish of stir-fried glass noodles and vegetables that is popular in Korean cuisine. The noodles are mixed with assorted vegetables, meat, and mushrooms, and seasoned with soy sauce and sesame oil.

Ingredients

  • 250g / 8.8 ounces of Korean sweet potato starch noodles
  • 100g / 3.5 ounces of rib eye fillet
  • 1 carrot (120g / 4.2 ounces)
  • 110g / 3.9 ounces of baby spinach
  • 1/4 of red capsicum / bell pepper (50g / 1.8 ounces)
  • 1/2 of yellow onion (105g / 3.7 ounces)
  • 100g / 3.5 ounces of fresh shiitake mushroom
  • 1/4 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 5 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp rice wine (mirin)
  • 3/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • cooking oil
  • fine sea salt
  • water
